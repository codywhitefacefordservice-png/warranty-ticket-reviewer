Search tricks
Type anything — it digs through the whole record: ticket text, checklist, risk flags, write-ups, appeal letters, and recall campaigns.
By number: just type it — 348211 finds that RO, a 17-character VIN finds that truck. Or be explicit: ro:348211 vin:1FTEW1EP5KFA22871
Exact phrase: "degas bottle" — words must appear together.
Scores: score<50 score>=80 — find your rough tickets or your clean ones.
Dates: from:7/1 to:7/15 on:yesterday from:july — or use the dropdown.
Combine everything: f-150 coolant score<70 from:june type:review
Multiple words must ALL match. If nothing matches all of them, it automatically shows records matching ANY of them, ranked by relevance.
No records matched every word — showing the closest matches (any word), best first.
Stored on the tool's private disk, behind the access code. Results ranked by relevance, then newest first.
